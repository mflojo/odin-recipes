Fettuccine Carbonara

Description:

This version of carbonara uses fettuccine noodles and is a creamy, tasty, and simple recipe to follow!

If you want to serve up to 6 people in a short prep & cook time, this is the recipe for you.

Ingredients:

  • 5 teaspoons olive oil
  • 4 shallots, diced
  • 1 pound bacon, cut into strips
  • 1 large onion, cut into thin strips
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 (16oz) package dry fettuccine pasta
  • 3/4 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 3 egg yolks
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large heavy saucepan over medium heat. Saute shallots until softened. Stir in bacon and onion; cook and stir until bacon is evenly browned. Stir in garlic when bacon is about half done. Remove from heat.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 mins or until al dente. Drain pasta, then return it to the pot.
  3. Whisk parmesan, cream, and egg yolks together in a medium bowl. Pour bacon mixture over pasta; stir in cream mixture. Season with salt and pepper.
