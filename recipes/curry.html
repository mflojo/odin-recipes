Japanese Curry

Description:

This hearty and warm meal will heal your soul and warm up your heart.

This type of curry is more mild compared to Indian and Thai curry, so it's perfect for introducing to friends.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 3/4 pounds beef chuck, cut into 2 inch cubes
  • 3 onions, quartered
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 1/2 tsp Worcetershire sauce
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste (optional)
  • water to cover
  • 4 carrots, cut into 3inch chunks
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon (optional)
  • 3 medium potatoes, cut into 3 inch chunks
  • 1 1/2 (3.5oz) containers Japanese curry roux, or more to taste
